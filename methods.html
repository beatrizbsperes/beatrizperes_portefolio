Course Description

Methods in Spatial Analysis course provides a comprehensive introduction to advanced geospatial techniques using ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Pro. Students gain hands-on experience working with spatial data to perform various analyses, including spatial analysis for understanding geographic patterns, visibility analysis to assess line-of-sight and viewsheds, spatial interpolation to estimate values across surfaces and terrain analysis to study landforms and elevation.

This course emphasizes practical applications of these methods, equipping participants with the technical skills and analytical approaches needed to solve real-world spatial problems effectively.

Finding the 15 closest Viewpoints in Salzburg

Salzburg is known for its beautiful mountains and amazing viewpoints. Defining a home address, the main point of this analysis was to find the 15 closest viewpoints. This is a very simple analysis but still very usefull.
